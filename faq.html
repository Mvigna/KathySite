FAQ

What is Life Coaching?

Life coaching is a process that involves guiding individuals to achieve personal or professional goals. Coaches use tools and techniques to help clients clarify their goals and take actionable steps to achieve them.

How long does each coaching session last?

Coaching sessions typically last 60-90 minutes, depending on the needs and goals of the client.

Do you offer remote coaching sessions?

Yes! I offer coaching sessions over phone, video call, or email.
